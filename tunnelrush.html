Tunnel Rush is a thrilling 3D obstacle course game that requires lightning-fast reflexes and excellent spatial awareness. The game's objective is simple: players must navigate through an endless tunnel filled with challenging obstacles, including spinning blades, moving barriers, and narrow passages. The game's controls are intuitive and easy to learn, but mastering them requires practice and skill. As players progress through the tunnel, the obstacles become more challenging and the speed increases, making the game even more challenging and exciting.
