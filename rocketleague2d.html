Rocket League is a popular video game developed and published by Psyonix. It combines soccer and vehicular combat in a fast-paced, physics-based multiplayer game. Players control rocket-powered cars and try to hit a ball into their opponents' goal while defending their own goal. The game includes various modes, such as 1v1, 2v2, 3v3, and 4v4, as well as various competitive and casual playlists. Rocket League also offers a wide range of customization options for the cars, including different bodies, wheels, and paint jobs. The game has gained a large following since its release in 2015 and has been praised for its addictive gameplay, exciting matches, and impressive graphics.
