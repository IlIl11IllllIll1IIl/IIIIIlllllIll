"Paper Flight" is a free online game where you control a paper airplane flying through various obstacles and collecting coins to upgrade your plane. The game features simple yet challenging gameplay, with easy-to-learn controls that allow you to make precise movements and maneuvers. As you progress through the levels, the obstacles become more difficult and the coins become harder to reach, making it a fun and engaging experience for players of all skill levels. Overall, "Paper Flight" is a great game for anyone looking for a quick and entertaining way to pass the time.
